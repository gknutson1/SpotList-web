How It Works



SpotList is a new way to create playlists you actually want to hear! Simply:





  1. Login with your Spotify account
  2. Begin to construct a list of rules for your playlist
  3. When you're satisfied with your rules, hit "Generate" and SpotList will begin creating the perfect playlist for you
  4. Save the playlist to your Spotify account and share your playlist others

Track Properties





  1. Artist. Stuff about Artists.
  2. Genre. Stuff about Genres.
  3. Year. Stuff about Years.
  4. Popularity. Stuff about Popularity.
  5. Length. Stuff about Length.
  6. Recent. Stuff about recent tracks.
